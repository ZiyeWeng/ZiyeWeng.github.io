Ziye Weng is currently a PhD student at the School of International and Public Affairs and Assistant Researcher at Shanghai Research Center for Innovation and Policy Evaluation in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (advised by [Prof. Bangcheng Liu]( https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=QpXvo34AAAAJ&hl=en)), having graduated MA in public policy and management at the City University of Hong Kong (advised by [Prof. Bangcheng Liu]( https://scholars.cityu.edu.hk/en/persons/ychandra/)). 


His research primarily focuses on behavioural public administration, with a particular interest in public service motivation, collaborative governance and digital governance. 

His research methodology expertise includes natural language processing and machine learning. His research has been published in Government Information Quarterly.
